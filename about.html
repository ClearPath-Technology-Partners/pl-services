Who We Are

Started in Sandusky.
Still here.

We are a service-based company out of Sandusky, Ohio. The services we offer are landscaping and lawn care, mobile car detailing, moving services, garbage bin cleaning, and gutter cleaning. At PL Services we stand on reliable and efficient business with affordable prices.

We service Erie County, Lorain County, Ottawa County, Cuyahoga County, and Huron County. Since 2021, we've been committed to delivering quality work that our customers can trust.
